常用GOOGLE搜索技巧
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|      需求        |       操作符       |            举例             |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|       与         |        空格        |       keyword1 keyword2     |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|       非         |         -          |   wantword - unwantedword   |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|       或         |         OR         |      keyword1 OR keyword2   |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|    站内搜索      |        site        |   keyword site:site_address |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|  完全匹配搜索    |         ""         |          "keywords"         |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
| 搜索特定类型文件 |      filetype      |      keyword filetype:pdf   |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|  URL包含关键字   |  inurl: allinurl:  |        inurl:keyword        |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|网页标题包含关键字| intitle, allintitle|       intitle:keyword       |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|  从缓存中查询    |       cache        |        cache:keyword        |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
|强制搜默认过滤的词|         +          |       +how, +what, +where   |
+------------------+--------------------+-----------------------------+
